Spaghetti Carbonara

A bowl of pasta with tomato and broccoli on top

Carbonara is a pasta dish made with fatty cured pork, hard cheese, eggs, salt, and black pepper. It is typical of the Lazio region of Italy

The dish took its modern form and name in the middle of the 20th century, the cheese used is usually pecorino romano.

Ingredients

  • Spaghett
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • pancetta
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti until al dente
  2. Fry pancetta until crisp
  3. Beat eggs and mix with grated Parmesan
  4. Toss hot pasta with pancetta and remove from heat
  5. Stir in the egg-cheese mixture quickly to make a creamy sauce, add pepper
