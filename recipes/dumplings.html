Dumplings

Momo, a cherished Nepali treat, features bite-sized dumplings filled with spiced minced meat, encased in delicate dough

Dumplings are a broad class of dishes that consist of pieces of cooked dough, often wrapped around a filling

The dough can be based on bread, wheat or other flours, or potatoes, and it may be filled with meat, fish, tofu, cheese, vegetables, or a combination

Ingredients

  • Ground pork or chicken
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Dumpling wrappers

Steps

  1. Mix meat, chopped cabbage, and seasonings
  2. Place a spoonful in each wrapper, fold and seal
  3. Steam, boil, or pan-fry until cooked. Serve with dipping sauce
