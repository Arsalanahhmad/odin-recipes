Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken tikka masala, cooked marinated chicken in spiced curry sauce

Chicken tikka masala is a dish consisting of roasted marinated chopped chicken (chicken tikka) in a spiced sauce (masala)

The sauce is usually creamy and orange-coloured. The origins of the dish are debated, with many believing it was created by South Asian cooks in Britain.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Yogurt
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Garam masala
  • Tomato puree
  • Cream
